INTRODUCTION

This article seeks to explore challenges and possible solutions undergraduates face in Nigerian universities. Who will solve these problems is a matter of perspective. Steven Covey teaches that one party can create a ‘Win-Win’ situation. This is true in the sense that if the Government decides to go for a win-win, the management will cooperate because it is also a win for them. The same thing applies to the management and students’ relationship. In summary, The Government, Management, and students all have a part to play in solving or at least minimizing these challenges whether individually or cooperatively if it will be a win for all parties.
